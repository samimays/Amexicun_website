About the Company

Amexicun has been in the food industry for 15+ years. When the owner, Leo Espitia, started bartending in Houston, he had no idea what was in store. During the start of COVID hitting on March 16th, 2020, he was working at three different bars. After the shutdown, he spent most of his time cooking and fell in love. Finally when the Texas laws stated a bar could be open at 51%, he jumped on this love, and pithed the idea.

At first, he was working with Ranch + Kitchen, since they desparately needed it to stay afloat. Once they saw the success, other bars such as Dino's Den and Public House hopped on board.
