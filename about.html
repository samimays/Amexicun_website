About the Company

We have been in the industry for 15+ years. When I started bartending in Houston, I had no idea what was in store for my future. I went back to it after I had taken a job in merchandise management for 5 years. That job didn’t suit my artistic vision nor did it make me happy. When covid-19 hit on March 16th 2020 I was working at 3 different bars at the time. After the shut down we quarantined for months and all I did was cook. I loved cooking but rarely had the time to do it anymore. We also had no money coming in because everything was shut down. Even restaurants!

Finally the law stated in order to open you had to be at 51% in food to open your doors. So I pitched my menu and idea to 3 bars. The Ranch Bar + Kitchen ran with it because they desperately needed to open. Quickly people noticed we were not playing around. Dino's Den got on board and we recently have been doing pop-ups in the Heights like Public House Heights HTX...Everyone had always told me that I needed to open my own restaurant one day! NOW I had time to do it, plus have the opportunity to help get our bars back open. That “one day” finally came when I had time to lay it all out and focus on the steps I needed to execute.

This journey has literally been in the making my whole life. I knew that I could cook, I knew I could plate and I knew I could deliver customer service to its full potential. I needed time to cultivate what my vision was, and deliver a product that you could only get from me. Create a fusion that was fun, new, and exciting that represented me. I needed something that would give me a sense of success. Not working for someone else, building their dream, but building mine. At that point AMEXICUN was born. I felt I was finally on to something as soon as I created the name. I am thrilled to say that after the hard work and sleepless nights manifesting my dream, it is finally a reality. We sold out our very first STEAK NIGHT tonight! After 4 months of building it! Thank you to everyone for everything you do for AMEXICUN! The Support, The Love & The Goodtimes. -God Bless- #gratitude in the #magnitude feeling #accomplished
