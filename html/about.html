About the Company

The Amexicun team has been in the industry for 15+ years. When the owner, Leo Espitia, started bartending in Houston, he had no idea what was in store for his future. He came back to bartending after having a job in merchandise management for 5 years. That job didn’t suit his artistic vision, nor did it make him happy.

When covid-19 hit on March 16th, 2020, Leo was working at 3 different bars at the time. After the shut down, everyone quarantined for months, but all Leo did was cook. He loved cooking but rarely had the time to do it anymore.

Finally the law stated in order for bars and restaurants to open, 51% of their profits had to come from food to open their doors. This is when Leo decided to pitch his menu and idea to 3 bars. The Ranch Bar + Kitchen ran with it first because they desperately needed to open. Quickly people noticed Leo and his team were not playing around. Dino's Den got on board as well, and they recently have been doing pop-ups in the Heights, like Public House Heights HTX.

Many people in Leo's life always told him that he needed to open his own restaurant one day. Now, he had time to, plus has the opportunity to help get our bars neighborhood bars back open! That “one day” finally came when he had time to lay it all out and focus on the steps needed to execute his dream.

Leo states that his journey has literally been in the making his whole life. He knew that he could cook, plate, and deliver customer service to its full potential. He needed time to cultivate his vision, and deliver a product that was unique to him. Create a fusion that was fun, new, and exciting that represented him. He needed something that would give him a sense of succes: not working for someone else, but building his own dream. At that point AMEXICUN was born. He felt he was finally on to something as soon as hecreated the name.

After the hard work and sleepless nights manifesting his dream, he is thrilled to say it is finally a reality! The Amexicun team sold out their very first STEAK NIGHT after just 4 months of building it!

The team is thankful for everyone and everything they do for AMEXICUN! Showing their love, support, and creating good memories!
